# FF AD5X Carriage model for Bambulab heater

Two versions - with a whole base and lightweight.
Used th Ash:
M3x4x4.5 - 7 pcs. (fake and heater fastening)
M3x5x4.5 - 4 pcs. (Printer carriage fastening)

Magnet 5x3 mm - 1 pc.

The Bambulab heater connector has a different spinning, the installation requires a cut of a connector from the FF heater (photos 3 and 4 Bambulab connector)

Print with heat-resistant materials
